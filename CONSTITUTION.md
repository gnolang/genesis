# gno.land Minimal Constitution

## Article 1: Establishment

This Minimal Constitution establishes the initial governance framework for gno.land.

## Article 2: T1 Governance

The T1 members shall serve as the initial governing body of gno.land with full authority to make decisions on behalf of the platform.

## Article 3: Constitutional Mandate

T1 members shall draft and ratify a complete Constitution for gno.land within three (3) months following the platform launch.

## Article 4: Temporary Authority

Until the complete Constitution is ratified, T1 members shall have the authority to:
- Make all governance decisions by simple majority vote
- Establish operational procedures as needed
- Take any actions necessary for the platform's operation and security

## Article 5: Transition

Upon ratification of the complete Constitution, this Minimal Constitution shall be superseded and all governance shall transition to the new framework.
